ARTHUB

ARTHUB is an innovative Web-Based Art Gallery Management and Exhibition Platform tailored to the specific needs of the Kenyan art community. We uncover insights from art galleries, artists, and enthusiasts through a mixed-methods approach to inform ARTHUB's design. ARTHUB emerges as a user-centric solution, uniting art stakeholders and revolutionizing art engagement, curation, and management.

Our Vision

To provide diverse and curated collection: The art gallery features a diverse range of artworks, representing different styles, periods, and mediums. The collection is carefully curated to ensure high-quality and meaningful pieces that captivate and inspire our clients.

Our Mission

ARTHUB serves the nation by welcoming all people to explore and experience art, creativity on the click of a button at the comfort of your office,couch or bed.

Our impact on the society

Exhibition and Promotion: ARTHUB serves as virtual exhibition spaces, allowing artists to display their artwork to a wider audience beyond physical gallery walls. It promotes artists and their works, facilitating exposure and recognition. Sales and Art Market: it enables art sales and transactions, connecting collectors and buyers with artists. It greatly contributes to the art market's growth and accessibility by facilitating the purchase of artworks worldwide. Artistic Collaboration: it facilitates collaborations between artists, curators, and art professionals, creating opportunities for artists to connect, share ideas, and collaborate on projects, fostering a vibrant artistic community.

About ARTHUB

ARTHUB is an artist-started gallery that aims at providing art lovers with first hand artwork experience like no other.

The driving force behind the idea was to provide first class art exploration experience to art lovers at the comfort of their homes,offices and absolutely anywhere they felt comfortable to view the artworks.
